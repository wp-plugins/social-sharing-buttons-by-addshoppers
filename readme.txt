== Description ==

This free plugin installs smart social sharing buttons that allow your visitors to easily share content on your WordPress site. These sharing buttons are tied to powerful analytics that allow you to track sharing activity and identify the influencers that are sending you traffic.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `wp-addshoppers.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Done!

If you want to configure the social networks that are included in your sharing buttons, go to the plugin settings page (Settings -> AddShoppers) where you can choose the networks that you want. 

<h4>Want to see the social sharing analytics for your site?</h4>
1. If you haven't already, create your free [AddShoppers account](http://www.addshoppers.com).
2. In your AddShoppers account, go to Settings -> Shops.
3. Scroll down and copy the Shop ID for your site.
4. Paste your Shop ID in the AddShoppers settings page in WordPress (it's under the Settings menu).

== Frequently asked questions ==

<h4>What if I want to use other sharing buttons than the default ones?</h4>
No problem! Just uncheck the "use default sharing buttons" box in the plugin options (under Settings > AddShoppers). 

Then, grab the code for the sharing buttons that you want in your [AddShoppers dashboard](https://www.addshoppers.com/merchants) under Apps > Sharing Buttons and paste in the desired location in your active theme.

<h4>How do I configure my Social Rewards?</h4>
This is done from within your [AddShoppers dashboard](https://www.addshoppers.com/merchants). Go to Apps > Social Rewards and set up your reward!
